Defining Information Systems Information systems are systematic, organized frameworks that offer a wide range of tools, procedures, and technologies that are used especially to gather, store, process, and distribute information within and between businesses. These systems are vital in converting unprocessed data into knowledge that can be used, acting as the basic framework for contemporary enterprises. Information systems are necessary to streamline operational procedures and enable well-informed decision-making.
