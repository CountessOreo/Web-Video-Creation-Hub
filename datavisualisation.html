Data Visualization

In information systems, data visualization is a graphical representation, either through charts, graphs, infographics, or animations that depict complicated data relationships. It is essential for converting unstructured data into insightful understandings that facilitate business decision-making by simplifying complex datasets. ​(IBM, 2023)​
